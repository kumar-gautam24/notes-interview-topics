```
#### Day -2: The Mental Preparation

**Morning (3 hours):**

**Understanding the Psychology:**

Read and internalize these concepts:

**1. The Dip (by Seth Godin concept):**
- Every skill has a "dip" - the period where it's hard and progress is slow
- Week 2-4 will be the hardest (novelty wears off, progress slows)
- Week 6-8 will be second hardest (concepts get complex)
- Most people quit in the dip. You won't.

**2. The Compound Effect:**
- 1% improvement daily = 37x better in a year
- You won't feel progress daily, but monthly progress will shock you

**3. The Two-Minute Rule:**
- On days you don't want to study: "Just do 2 minutes"
- 2 minutes turns into 20 minutes 80% of the time
- Showing up is 80% of success

**4. The Identity Shift:**
- Stop saying: "I'm a Flutter developer learning Go"
- Start saying: "I'm a software engineer who builds full-stack mobile systems"
- Your identity shapes your actions

**Create Affirmations (say daily, morning):**
```
1. "I complete what I start."
2. "I am becoming a great engineer."
3. "Discomfort is growth."
4. "I learn from every mistake."
5. "I am building my future."
